These field catalogues are offered independently of the NOW database. Note that they are simply raw field data as recorded during the project, and have not been updated. For current information, please refer to the NOW database and published lists.

This page is still being developed and other catalogues will be added in the future. Please contact Mikael Fortelius if you have data which you would like to preserve and present here.
